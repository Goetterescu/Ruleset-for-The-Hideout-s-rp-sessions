Roleplaying Guideline

  • §1
    This is a roleplaying server, be mature, and don't fool around like it's a kindergarten.
  • §2
    Keep it real, we appreciate the cruelty of this alternative reality. There are no excuses when your car breaks down, and you must walk to the next repair shop.
  • §3
    Do not teleport, or node grab too much, you can just reverse your car into the parking spot.
  • §4
    This saying translated from German: No plaintiff, no judge. Rules can be broken as long as everyone's okay with that.
  • §5
    You are allowed to swear but do not insult someone, out of the roleplaying realms.
  • §6
    Roleplaying ends where reality begins - stop if someone's not feeling like continuing the scene. Don't force scenarios on other players.
