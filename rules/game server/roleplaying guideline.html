Roleplaying Guideline

Terms

Here are some terms that you'll hear all the time, and what they mean.
  • TP
    Teleportation is when a player uses the map [TAB key] to move to another location. Holding the respawn button pressed and moving your car around falls under TP as well.
  • IC / OOC
    In-character & Out-of-character. These two terms describe if you're acting as your character or outside of the rp-realm.
  • Node Grabbing
    The usage of RoR's tool to grab a node of any object and move it around.
  • PD / FD / MD Our three important departments. The police, fire and medical department.
  • Scenes Every time something happens that can be played out (like a shopping tour with a friend; a house fire; bar brawl) we call it a scene. These scenes are what moves our event's and make them so fun. Some scenes can be generated by the Staff and will be publicly announced during an event.

General Rules

  • Additional Rules
    Please keep in mind that this is a second-party event. We are still operating under the flag and the rules of The Hideout!
  • FRP
    Fail role play is the act of failing to play out a scene. This term is very generic and can be split up into multiple sections, which all fall under this rule.
    • OOC
      Falsely formatted OOC-talk is not allowed. Please use () if you want to chat out of character.
    • MCRP
      Missing crash role play describes the act of ignoring a car crash. Please be rational and do anything from at least inspecting the damage, up to playing out to be very injured.
    • Pocket-RP
      Pocket role play is not allowed. If you want to use a very large object, like a ladder, you will have to get it from somewhere logical first (for example the trunk of your car).
    • Forced-RP
      You can not force a scene onto someone. Give players a chance to control the scene as well. For example, play out a heated argument like this:
      Player 1: Watch your mouth! *swings with his fist towards you*
      Player 2: Ey, what is wrong with you *narrowly ducks away*
      Instead of just using something like *Punches you and breaks your nose*.
    • Node Grabbing & TP
      Players are not allowed to use the node grabber or teleport their car or character for comfort.
      If you wrecked your car you will have to walk to the repair shop; if you are hooking up a trailer, you will need to do that manually; if you can't reverse a trailer, you will need to learn that skill.
      Note that you can use the node grabber if it's vital for the scene to do so (like when the FD is prying open the door of your car after a wreck).
  • Scene Voiding
    Staff can void scenes if necessary. This usually happens when a unrealistic or impossible scene has occured. Voiding will be publicly announced in-game and players need to delete everything related to the scene and forget about it.
  • Combat Logging
    Players are not allowed to leave the server during a scene. IRL emergencies are obviously an excpetion. But please let the other players in the scene know that you need to go (OOC-chat), and give them a few moments to sort the situation in-game.
  • Usage of Money
    This can be currently ignored, since we don't have a functional Discord-Bot for this.
    Only the currency from our Discord server can be used.
  • Forbidden Items and Jobs
    Only Staff can use police cars and role play for the police.
  • Meta Gaming
    We don't usually monitor this, but we might do so one day.
    Your character can not read the chat like you are doing, keep that in mind. For example, your character can not know that there's a police officer on the other side of the town.
  • RDM
    The act of attacking a player with no rational background is forbidden.
