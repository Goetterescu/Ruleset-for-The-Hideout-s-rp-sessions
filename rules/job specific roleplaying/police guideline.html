Police Guideline

  • §1
    Vehicles of the Police are only considered as such if they're meeting the following requirements. Only then, they can be used for the job.
    • 1
      They have to be equipped with their specific lighting.
    • 2
      The vehicle or trailer has to approved by the server staff. Here you can view the list of allowed police vehicles and trailers.
    • 3
      The combination has to meet all paragraphs of the VCR.
  • §2
    These codes have to be forwarded to the chief of the Police department before the driver merges into a public road or turns on his lights/horns.
  • §3
    Police officers are allowed to defunction other combinations if needed.
  • §4
    All legal actions have to be reported to the active chief of the department and later approved. The violation system has not been created, yet - so the active chief can request violation points to be charged on players if needed. Server staff will have the last word in that case. Chiefs can also create their own systems for violation points and fines, this will need a final approval from server staff as well.
  • §5
    Police officers need to keep an realistic radius to the suspect whenever they are collection evidence for an infraction. You can not spot a speeding car from 2 kilometers and around three corners, keep it real.
  • §6
    Communication is key. All actions have to be forwarded to other officers while on duty. Please use these codes.
