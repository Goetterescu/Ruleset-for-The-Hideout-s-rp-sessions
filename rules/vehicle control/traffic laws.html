Traffic Laws

    Uncontrolled Intersections
  • UI1
    At uncontrolled intersections, drivers have to yield to the first vehicle to arrive at the intersection. Vehicles must use the intersection in the order in which they arrived.
    • UI1.1
      If multiple vehicles arrive at roughly the same time, you have to yield to the driver on your right-hand side.

    General
  • G1
    Always, unless otherwise specified, yield to upcoming traffic from the front, before you make a left hand turn.
  • G2
    Traffic signs can overwrite any general traffic laws, expect for the emergency services section, as long as they make sense where they are.
  • G3
    Crosswalks have to be kept clear, do not park 10 meters before and after them and don't stop on them, unless it's unpreventable.
  • G4
    Traffic lights are not synchronized between players. For this reason, drivers have to treat them as stop signs.
  • G5
    Vehicles are never allowed to be driven in a manner, which might endanger others and public or personal property, even that of the respective driver.
  • G6
    Vehicles and combinations with a total weight of over 3.5 tons have to stay on the right lane at any time, unless otherwise ordered, when traveling on highways.
  • G7
    The use of hazard lights, horns and lights should not be abused.
  • G8
    Drivers have use common sense at all times. This might even require to "ignore" certain laws, to insure the safety of others and the flow of traffic.
  • G9
    All traffic has to use the right lane of the road.
  • G10
    Vehicles and combinations with a total weight of over 3.5 tons are not allowed to overtake other vehicles on rural roads or intercity.

    • Parking
  • P1
    It's not allowed to park on main or priority roads.
  • P2
    It's not allowed to park on highways, or 10 meters next to a highway.
  • P3
    Parking on roads is only allowed if you're not slowing down the traffic flow.
  • P4
    Vehicles and combinations with a weight of over eight tons are not allowed to park on sidewalks.
  • P5
    Entrances and intersections are never allowed to be blocked.

    • Speeds
  • S1
    The speed limit intercity is 60 km/h (about 40 mph).
  • S2
    On rural roads you can only drive 100 km/h (about 62 mph).
  • S3
    There is no speed limit for highways.
  • S4
    Vehicles can have special speed limitations ordered by the police department or the owner of the vehicle, drivers have to respect those limits. These limits can never be greater than the speed allowed in the speeds section of the traffic laws.
  • S5
    Vehicles and combinations with a total weight of over 3.5 tons are not allowed to exceed 70 km/h.
    • S5.1
      Vehicles and combinations with a total weight of over 30 tons are not allowed to exceed 50 km/h.

    Emergency Services
  • E1
    Police officers are allowed to stop other traffic members without a special reason. Traffic filters (mobile sections which can be put on any road, expect highways, to stop and check drivers, their combinations and passengers) can be set up at any given time. All traffic has to stop for these filters, even without being instructed to do so by the police.
    • E1.1
      Without a filter, drivers have to stop for police officers if they are being clearly instructed to do so.
  • E2
    Emergency services have the right of way in the case of an emergency.
