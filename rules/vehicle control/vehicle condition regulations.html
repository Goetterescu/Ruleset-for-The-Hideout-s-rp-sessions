VCR

    Liability
  • L1
    Not road safe vehicles are considered a threat and might cause accidents. This means that they can be blamed for any case which involves such vehicles. In order to not be classified as "not road safe", a vehicle has to meet every paragraph of the VCR.

    • Combinations
  • C1
    Loads are not allowed to be taller as the truck or trailer, whichever is greater.
  • C2
    Loads are not allowed to stick out more than 2 meters, about a player length, over the rear lights of the vehicle/trailer.
    • C2.1
      Ramps and doors of truck/trailer are allowed to be extended and used to hold loads. In that case: the end of the door/ramp plus 2 meters would be the new max. loading area. In short: ramps make part of the loading area.
  • C3
    Vehicles (not trailers) are required to be equipped with head and rear lights, as well as brake lights or blinkers. There is no need for blinkers as long as there are brake lights on the rear.
    • C3.1
      All required lights have to be undamaged and visible.
  • C4
    Parts of the vehicle or trailer are not allowed to reach out of the vehicle's or trailer's factory dimensions. This is to prevent unwanted damage to external objects and people.

    • Loads
  • L1
    Loads have to be secured either with ropes or enclosed areas, like vans.

    • Trailers
  • T1
    Trailers of all kinds have to be able to move freely around the hook.
